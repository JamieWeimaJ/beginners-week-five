The modern Japanese writing system uses a combination of kanji (adopted Chinese characters) and kana (syllabic Japanese scripts). Kana consists of two different scripts: hiragana and katakana. This website is a soundboard of the hiragana and katakana scripts. Click on any kana to hear how it's pronounced!
